# tym2muv - User Manual

## Welcome to tym2muv!
**tym2muv** is your premier real estate marketplace for Africa. Whether you're looking for a new home or listing a property for sale or rent, this guide will help you get the most out of our platform.

---

## For Property Seekers (Buyers & Renters)

### 1. Finding Your Perfect Property
*   **Search Bar:** Use the search bar on the home page to enter a location or keyword.
*   **Filter Options:** Tap the "Filters" icon to narrow down by price, bedrooms, property type (Apartment, House, etc.), and more.
*   **Change Country:** Tap the country flag in the top bar to switch between Ghana, Nigeria, Kenya, and other supported regions.

### 2. Viewing Property Details
*   **Card Flip:** Tap a property card to see a quick summary of the seller's information.
*   **Detailed View:** Click the property title or image to open the full details page.
*   **Gallery:** Swipe through the high-resolution images to get a better feel for the space.
*   **Virtual Tour:** If available, click the "Virtual Tour" link to explore the property in 3D.

### 3. Contacting Sellers
*   **In-App Chat:** Click the "Chat" button on any listing to message the seller directly.
*   **WhatsApp:** Use the WhatsApp icon to start a conversation on your mobile device.
*   **Direct Call:** Tap the "Call" button to dial the seller's number. *Note: Always follow our safety tips when meeting sellers.*

---

## For Property Owners & Agents (Sellers)

### 1. Posting a New Ad
*   **Sign In:** You must be signed in to post an ad.
*   **Post Ad Button:** Tap the "Post Ad" button in the bottom navigation bar.
*   **Property Details:** Fill in the title, price, location, and property type.
*   **Upload Photos:** Add up to 10 high-quality photos to make your listing stand out.
*   **Premium Option:** Toggle "Premium Listing" to get 91 days of visibility and priority ranking in search results.

### 2. Managing Your Listings
*   **Profile Page:** Go to your profile to see all your active and pending listings.
*   **Editing:** Click the "Edit" button on any of your listing cards to update the price, description, or photos.
*   **Expiry:** Standard ads stay live for 35 days. Premium ads stay live for 91 days. You can renew them before they expire.

### 3. Building Trust
*   **Complete Your Profile:** Add a professional avatar and a short bio to attract more inquiries.
*   **Verification:** Contact support to get your "Verified" badge, which significantly increases buyer trust.

---

## Safety Tips
*   **Meet in Public:** Always meet sellers or agents in a safe, public place for property viewings.
*   **No Upfront Payments:** Never pay any booking fees or deposits before seeing the property and verifying the seller's identity.
*   **Report Suspicious Activity:** If a listing looks too good to be true or a seller behaves suspiciously, use the "Report" feature.
